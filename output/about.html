About MenuDB

Hi, we’re two Chinese-American cousins, Jocelyn and John. We both love food but suck at reading Chinese. When we walk into a Chinese restaurant, they look at us and hand us the Chinese menu, and then we have to ask for the “normal” menu. This is our admittedly nerdy effort to help us make sense of “ethnic” food so we (and you) can start ordering The Good Stuff outside of our little knowledge bubbles.

  • Google Translate is great for what it does, but it’s not trained on restaurant menus, and it doesn’t know dialectal meanings.
  • We do want to cover other cuisines, but we’re starting with Chinese because 1) it’s what we know and 2) it’s probably the hardest (in terms of both culinary and linguistic complexity).
  • Many years ago, Jocelyn made a Memrise course to learn to read Chinese menus.

Comments, corrections, ideas? We’d love to hear from you. Email us at hello@food-tbd.com.
